SERVICE.md

Community service & leadership I have been involved with
  • Namma Trees

    Worked in conjunction with the Bengaluru Metro Rail Corporation Limited & the Karnataka Forest Department to help incentivize Bengaluru residents to participate in afforestation. Created a website where citizens could mark where they had planted a new tree, along with their name. As of January 2023, over 550 trees have been marked on the website.

  • RightToRead

    Created a service that collects school material (textbooks, notebooks, supplies, etc.) and effectively distributes the sourced material amongst rural libraries across Karnataka. Adopted by the government of Karnataka, along with other ventures, culminating in 1,500,000 students enrolled in the rural library system within Karnataka.

  • LEO Club Mantri Espana

    Lead organizer for fundraising events as a way to sustain our expenditures to noble charitable causes. Over the 4 years as the lead organizer, raised over $10,000 each year through walkathons, art exhibitions, sports tournaments, and newspaper collection drives.

  • Cheap Water Purifier

    Designed, created, & prototyped a cost-efficient water purifier that costs less than ₹3000 (>$40) that meets all potable water standards of TPS, pH level, electrical conductivity, and hardness.

  • Indus Lake Rejuvenation

    Led high school students through the process of the creation of a website from design, to wireframe, and development. Assisted in the blueprint development of seed-bombing drones. Ultimately, led to the passing of legislation protecting, surveying, and revitalizing Bidaraguppe Lake by the government of Karnataka.
